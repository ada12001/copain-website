What We Offer

Breakfast & Brunch

Morning Spreads

Pastry boxes, croissant platters, seasonal fruit boards, granola, yogurt, and housemade jams. Perfect for corporate breakfasts, team meetings, or weekend brunches.

Lunch & Corporate

Working Lunch

Sandwich platters on housemade baguettes, seasonal salads, quiches, charcuterie, and cheese boards. Individually packaged or family-style.

Private Dinners

Full-Service Events

Farm-to-table seated dinners, rehearsal dinners, and private celebrations. Multi-course French-inspired menus with full service staff and custom florals available.

Weddings

Wedding Catering

From rehearsal breakfasts to full reception dinners and tiered wedding cakes, we work with your vision to create a day rooted in beauty and food.

Holiday Orders

The Process

How It Works

01

Inquire

Submit your event details through Field Pea Catering — our preferred catering partner — including date, guest count, and event type.

02

Consult

Our team will reach out to discuss menu options, dietary needs, service style, and logistics. Every event is custom-built.

03

Confirm

Review your custom proposal, finalize details, and secure your date with a deposit. We handle everything from there.

04

Celebrate

We arrive, set up, and bring the Copain experience to your table. You focus on your guests — we handle the rest.
