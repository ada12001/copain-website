The Copain
Difference

Every loaf, baguette, and laminated pastry we produce for wholesale partners is made using the same methods we use for our own cases — long fermentation, stone deck ovens, no shortcuts. Our wholesale program is selective because we want to ensure that quality is consistent at every partner location.

We work with restaurants, hotels, specialty grocers, and corporate dining programs throughout the Charlotte metro and the Triad. Minimum orders and delivery schedules vary by product and partner.
